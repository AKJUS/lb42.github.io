United Kingdom, War Memorials, 1914-1949

Publication information MyHeritage

Narrative

The records primarily consist of soldiers from the First and Second World Wars with a few records from different wars. The number of British casualties was smaller in wars following World War II, and the number of records from other conflicts is consequently low.
